This is Us

The word Stratcom comes from a latin word 'Strata' and the English word 'Computer'.
Strata means fast and a Computer is obviously known to everyone.
So Stratcom is a place where Computer skills are taught and instilled into people of all ages in a record period of time.

That being said, Stratcom has a good reputation, experienced lecturers and also a conducive environment.
There is also free breakfast and a restaurant in case an intern feels the need to eat again later during the day.

Stratcom also offers a wide range of study times,for those that study during weekdays, one can choose morning (8:00am-12:pm) or afternoon (12:00pm-4:00pm) or even evening (5:00pm-9:00pm) while those that study over the weekend study from morning till evening only twice a week (on the weekends).
